Leadership
The operator
behind the capital.
Cole founded Partner Equity with a straightforward thesis: that the businesses most worth backing are rarely the ones with the loudest pitch — they're the ones with strong fundamentals and the right operator alongside them.
Before Partner Equity, Cole co-founded Bloom Health Partners, scaling it from inception to acquisition by a publicly traded healthcare technology firm in under 18 months. Prior to that, he spent years in institutional finance at JonesTrading and TPG Capital — where he learned what separates businesses that endure from those that don't.
He holds a BBA in Finance and Entrepreneurial Management, cum laude, from Texas Christian University — where he also earned Leadership Honors.
